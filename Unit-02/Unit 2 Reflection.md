# Unit 2 Reflection

[Reflect on your journey here]


Unit 2 was all about design thinking. I learned to plan a website by starting with the user's needs, not just the code. The steps are: understand the user, define the problem, brainstorm ideas, make a prototype, and test it. This process showed me that good websites are not just about looking pretty, but about being easy and clear for everyone to use. It changed how I think about building things for the web.
